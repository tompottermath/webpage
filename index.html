Recent Teaching:

Outreach: For several years I have gone to schools in HRM and throughout Nova Scotia to help promote math literacy and enthusiasm. I worked as presenter and content developer for NS Math Circles from 2018 to 2020, and as Director from August 2020 to June 2024. In summer 2024 I started as a lecturer at CBU, but have continued to visit schools in the CBRM for outreach and recruitment activities. This has been a fantastic and enriching opportunity for me.

Teaching Philosophy: I think that a good teacher, like a good coach, works with a person's natural abilities, and helps them to achieve their personal best.
